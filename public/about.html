Who We Are

Eastern NC's Most Trusted Tree Company

Godhans was founded by Michael Godbersen, a veteran who took the discipline and precision of military service and applied it to the tree care industry in Eastern North Carolina.

Since 2020, we've grown into one of the most trusted tree and outdoor service companies in the region — handling everything from routine trimming to large-scale land clearing and emergency response.

We believe every job deserves the same level of attention, regardless of size. That means showing up on time, doing the work right, and leaving your property cleaner than we found it — every single time.
